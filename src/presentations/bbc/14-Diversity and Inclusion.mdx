# BBC Interview - Diversity & Inclusion

Working with a diverse team is crucial to delivering products that are inclusive and accessible to everyone.

It's important to understand the different perspectives and experiences of your team, and to be aware of the impact of your own biases and assumptions.

When leading people you're responsible for creating an environment where everyone feels safe and supported to do their best work.

This means meeting people where they are, and being open to learning from them.
